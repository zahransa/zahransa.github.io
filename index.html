Research Highlights

Performance Analysis of Optically Pumped 4He Magnetometers vs. Conventional SQUIDs
Zahran, S., et al. (Sensors, 2022)
[Link to paper]
We compared OPMs to conventional SQUIDs for MEG acquisition across head models from infants to adults. Our analysis demonstrates the improved spatial resolution and flexibility of OPM-based systems, especially for pediatric neuroimaging.
Performance of Source Imaging Techniques of Spatially Extended Generators of Uterine Activity
Zahran, S., et al. (Informatics in Medicine Unlocked, 2019)
[Link to paper]
This study evaluated different inverse methods for imaging spatially distributed uterine activity during pregnancy. The results highlight the potential of noninvasive electrophysiological imaging for clinical assessment of uterine contractions.
